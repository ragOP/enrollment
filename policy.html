Enrollment Policy

Welcome to our Enrollment Policy page. Please review the following guidelines to understand our enrollment process and requirements.

Eligibility

  • Applicants must meet the minimum age requirement.
  • All required documents must be submitted before the deadline.
  • Prerequisite qualifications must be fulfilled.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit all necessary documents.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend an interview if required.

Important Notes

  • Incomplete applications will not be processed.
  • All information provided must be accurate and truthful.
  • Admission decisions are final.

For further information, please contact our admissions office.
